**C-Eval: A Multi-Level Multi-Discipline Chinese Evaluation Suite for Foundation Models**

*Co-author*

Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He.

**Venue:** NeurIPS 2023

**Research Area:** Foundation Model Evaluation

This work presents C-Eval, a multi-level multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models, providing comprehensive evaluation benchmarks for Chinese language models.
