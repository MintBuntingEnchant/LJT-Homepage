**On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding**

*First author*

Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He.

**Venue:** Arxiv (2025)

**Research Area:** Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)

This work investigates the perception bottleneck of vision-language models (VLMs) for chart understanding, addressing key challenges in VLM hallucination.
